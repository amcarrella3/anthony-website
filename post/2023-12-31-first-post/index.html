First post!

 Posted on December 31, 2023  |  115 words  |  Anthony M. Carrella

Hello and welcome to my first blog post! This site is built with Hugo, a fast and efficient static site generator, and it’s designed using the SweetB Hugo theme (check it out on GitHub). This theme is a variant of the popular Beautiful Hugo (found here), and I’ve chosen it for its clean and modern design.

![Music listening history](/media/Screenshot 2024-01-01 at 20.00.37.png)

Behind the Scenes

I’ve selected a mix of Space Grotesk, Optima, and Lexend Deca fonts to ensure readability and a sleek look. The goal was to create a site that’s easy on the eyes and simple to navigate.

Thanks for dropping by, and I look forward to sharing more with you soon.
