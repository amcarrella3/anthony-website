I consider myself a contemplative leader, communicator, and creative. I partner with individuals and organizations and equip them to live into their fullest potential.

With a unique blend of design thinking, creative problem-solving, and lifelong learning, I develop pragmatic and harmonious solutions for community, communications, and creative initiatives.

- **CONTEMPLATIVE LEADERSHIP: I lead from a place of openness, deep listening, and stillness,** considering how my actions align with my intentions. Through daily contemplative practices, I cultivate and participate in environments where clarity and wisdom aid in making decisions and communicating with authenticity. By empowering others to lead with presence and purpose, I help organizations innovate, adapt, and thrive.

- **EMPATHY & NON-VIOLENT COMMUNICATION: I listen deeply to understand the needs, desires, and pinch points of the people and communities I serve.** By identifying with and celebrating the individuals and places I work with, I engage with their mission, values, and culture first-hand. This enables me to deeply relate to their strengths, opportunities, challenges, and aspirations. I create space for others to share non-violent and reflective dialogue, fostering cultures of trust and collaboration. I call this the active listening and immersive research process. Together, we'll partner to identify, label, and relate to these observations and develop a bold, clear, and compelling vision for their future. We'll ensure reforms are sustainable, scalable, and aligned with their long-term goals.

- **DESIGN THINKING:** I utilize an experiential, human-centered-design approach to organize information, identify opportunities, streamline processes, and create solutions that are beautiful and functional.

- **COLLABORATION:** I foster and encourage safe environments where creativity thrives, and every voice is heard and valued.

- **LIFE LONG LEARNING:** I am passionate about expanding my and others' knowledge and skills to meet new challenges with curiosity and adaptability.

Whether you're looking to reform, modernize, or embark on a new venture, I'm ready to guide or partner with you through the journey.
