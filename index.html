Leader, Communicator, Creator

I consider myself a contemplative leader, communicator, and creative. I partner with individuals and organizations and equip them to live into their fullest potential.

With a unique blend of design thinking, creative problem-solving, and lifelong learning, I develop pragmatic and harmonious solutions for community, communications, and creative initiatives.

✤

  • CONTEMPLATIVE LEADERSHIP — I lead from a place of openness, deep listening, and stillness, considering how my actions align with my intentions. Through a daily practice of inner work I cultivate and participate in environments where contemplation aids in making decisions and communicating authentically. My job is to help organizations innovate, adapt, and thrive by empowering others to lead with the same presence and purpose freely available for all of us to discern.

  • EMPATHY & NON-VIOLENT COMMUNICATION — I am deeply committed to immersing and attuning myself to the needs, desires, and aspirations of the people and places I serve. When engaging with an organization’s mission, values, and culture, my teams and I share a process of active listening and rich reflection. I partner with others to foster dynamic work environments where we co-create cultures of non-violent communication and creative collaboration. These ongoing partnerships of trust and empathy are rooted in our shared commitment to making an impact and bringing about meaningful change.

  • DESIGN THINKING — I utilize an experiential, human-centered-design approach to organize information, identify opportunities, streamline processes, and create solutions that are beautiful and functional.

  • COLLABORATION — I foster and encourage safe environments where creativity thrives, and every voice is heard and valued.

  • LIFE LONG LEARNING — I am passionate about expanding my and others’ knowledge and skills to meet new challenges with curiosity and adaptability.

✤

Whether you’re looking to reform, modernize, or embark on a new venture, I’m ready to guide or partner with you through the journey.
